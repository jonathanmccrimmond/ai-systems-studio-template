# GLOSSARY

- SSOT: Single Source of Truth; the canonical document that governs project state and decisions (`PROJECT_MASTER.md`).
- ADR: Architecture Decision Record; a dated record of an important technical or structural decision and its consequences.
- Agent: An AI role definition responsible for specific project tasks under documented constraints.
- Workstream: A coordinated lane of work (for example documentation, build, showcase) with its own priorities and outputs.
- Roadmap: The time-phased plan of milestones, priorities, and risks used to guide execution.
- Milestone: A significant checkpoint with clear outcomes used to measure progress.
- Showcase: Curated materials (often slides/demo narrative) used to present project progress and value.
- Portfolio: Public-facing project summary intended for professional positioning and credibility.
- Context Snapshot: A short status document that lets someone quickly understand project purpose, state, decisions, and next steps.
